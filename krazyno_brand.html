Who is KrazyNo

The name
behind the grind

My mother raised 7 kids on her own. She worked every single day — no complaints, no excuses. Watching her do that shaped everything about who I became. Video games kept me out of trouble growing up. They gave me focus, discipline, and a competitive edge I carry to this day.

But on the days she'd leave for work, I'd sneak out of the house — lace up my running shoes, throw on my shorts, and just run. Outside, in the open air. That feeling of freedom hit different. That's where my love for running, nature, and pushing my body was born.

What drives me every day to become stronger — to keep going, to keep building — is my mother. She gave everything for us. The least I can do is become everything I can be.
